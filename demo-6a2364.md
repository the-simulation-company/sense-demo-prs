# PLM connector: subscribe to Windchill/Teamcenter/Solidworks BOM change webhooks

Wire up change-data-capture for the PLM connectors (DRY-1218). Today's onboarding flow pulls each BOM once and never resubscribes; Hugo plans against stale BOM until someone manually re-pushes.

Changes:
- Windchill change-event webhook subscription on the connector handshake.
- Same pattern for Teamcenter and Solidworks PDM.
- On change event: invalidate cached BOM, re-run reorder logic for SKUs that joined or left the assembly.
- One-shot backfill script pulls the last 30 days of changes from the customer's Windchill audit log so anything still pending corrects.

Reported by Voss (Lena Köster) on a Fathom call after Hugo continued recommending a part engineering had removed from the BOM two weeks earlier.
